Market Sizing and Forecasting

TAM, SAM, SOM Analysis. Size up your addressable market the right way.

One of the first things investors ask is “what is your total addressable market”. You’ll finally be able to answer with confidence.

Why you need the service

  • You’re a startup founder preparing your pitch deck and you want to boost your credibility in front of seasoned investors. Perhaps you already pitched and got pushback on your projections.
  • You’re an entrepreneur considering expansion into new markets or launching a new products. Now you’re wondering if there is potential and how much you should invest.
  • You’re an investor or a fund manager trying to calculate the upside potential for a new deal you are considering.

The Process

The Process

01

Target audience sizing

We analyze your solution and its use cases to identify the perfect audience for your business so we can then size up the audience
02

Revenue model analysis

We dissect how your business generates cash from its customers. Then we connect those findings to size up the revenue potential across geographies and customer segments
03

Competitive analysis

Understanding the competitiveness in your niche and all entry barriers is crucial when calculating the obtainable market share (and SOM) of your business.
04

Growth projections

In the end, we analyze the industry’s projected growth rate and forecast your TAM, SAM, SOM, and obtainable market share over the next 5 years.
